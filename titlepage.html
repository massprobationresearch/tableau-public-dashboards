Massachusetts Probation Service Research Department

Welcome to the Massachusetts Probation Service Research Department Tableau dashboards. These dashboards are maintained by the Research Department to provide insights on the caseload and trends across Probation. Click on the court department below to view all the dashboards that are monthly or annually updated. The departments that currently have data dashboards are BMC, District, Juvenile, Superior, and Probate and Family Court. There are also dashboards for the Pretrial and GPS units. The "Overall/Other" category includes overall caseload and violation trends across all departments as well as data on Specialty courts.
